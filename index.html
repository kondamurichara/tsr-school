About our school & collage

Telanagana State Residential School & Collage (Boys)

TSRSC is an Autonomus Society by Government, Established in 1985 in about 29acres. It is only a school till 2018 after then they also added junior collage where it has 2 sections one is bipc and one is mpc.whereas in school the classes are from 5th to 10th. each class consists of one section and each class will have 40 students. Additionally, a certain number of seats will be reserved for the children of teachers, ensuring they too can benefit from the institution's quality education.In the event that a student decides not to pursue their studies in this particular school, they have the option to withdraw voluntarily.
